
# Customer Intelligence & Segmentation Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes customer demographics, loyalty behaviour, purchase activity, satisfaction, engagement risk, and segmentation opportunities. The goal is to help businesses understand their customers better and identify groups that require retention, growth, recovery, or win-back actions.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Customer Overview

Provides a high-level view of customer demographics, income tiers, satisfaction levels, purchase activity, preferred contact methods, and recency risk.

### 2. Loyalty & Engagement Analysis

Explores loyalty points, purchase history, customer tenure, feedback, and engagement status to identify active, lapsed, and high-value customer groups.

### 3. Customer Segments & Opportunities

Groups customers into strategic segments such as Champions, Loyal Core, Growth Opportunity, Service Recovery, and At-Risk / Win-back, with recommended business actions for each segment.
